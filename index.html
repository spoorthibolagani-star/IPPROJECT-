Alternative Proteins

Alternative proteins are non-traditional sources of protein that replace or reduce the use of animal-based proteins.

They are made from plants, microorganisms, or lab-grown cells.

They provide similar nutrients but are more sustainable and environment-friendly.

Why do we need Alternative Proteins?

✔ Growing population → more protein demand

✔ Traditional meat → pollution, water use, deforestation

✔ Health concerns → cholesterol & fats

✔ Ethical concerns → animal welfare
